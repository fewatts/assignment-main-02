This is our story...

Where are a group of friends with one big passion in common : cooking. So after many years on thinking what we where going to do in the future, in 2010, we take the risk and decided to create our own culinary company in which we share all our knowledge and passion to everyone who wants to learn recipes of all the world. We are glad and proud to say that our small company is growing and that our customers are still choosing our recipes for more than 10 years and that's is our biggest happiness.
